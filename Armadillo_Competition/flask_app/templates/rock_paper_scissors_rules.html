"Rock, Paper, Scissors"

Nothing special to this game; just the classic game of "Rock, Paper, Scissors!" You will select either Rock, Paper, or Scissors and I will do the same. Like the original: Rock crushes Scissors, Scissors cuts Paper, and Paper covers Rock. Your goal is to select the weapon that beats mine. If we tie, we'll go again, until a clear winner is decided. Failure to do so, will lead to your elmination. But your sucess will reward you 250pts!
