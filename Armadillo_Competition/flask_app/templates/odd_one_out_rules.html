"Odd One Out"

"One of these is not like the others!" Can you spot the diffrence between a grid of letters? I will show you a HUGE list of letters, all of 'em are the same... except for one! Your goal is to scan through the whole grid and find the "Odd One Out." Better be quick, cause in this game, nothing is too easy. I will be generious and give you 20 seconds to find him, but me being me, will only give you one guess to do so. On a final note, your answer should be in all CAPS for it to be accepted Failure to do so, will lead to your elmination. But your sucess will reward you 250pts!
