"Shuffle the E's"

This is exactllty like the old-school carninval game where they put a ball underneath a cup of a set and proceed to shuffle them around. The goal is you to keep an eye on the cup that contains the ball. Then they'll ask you to spot the cup that has the ball underneath it. Well the rules are the same here! You will see four lowercase e's and one UPPERCASE E. As I shuffle the letter around, your goal is to eye the UPPERCASE E. When the E's vanish, five numbers will appear. As expected, your goal is to slect the letter that has the UPPERCASE. Failure to do so, will lead to your elmination. But your sucess will reward you 250pts!
