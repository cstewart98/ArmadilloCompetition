"MasterMind"

Are you good a picking locks? Well for this game, you're going to need to be. I will ask for you to enter a 4-digit combination, then I will show you if what you said is correct. I will only show you the numbers that you got right. With what I provide you, you need to try and crack the combination. There is no time, so don't fret... HOWEVER! You will only have 9 attempts to crack the lock. Failure to do so, will lead to your elmination. But your sucess will reward you 250pts!
