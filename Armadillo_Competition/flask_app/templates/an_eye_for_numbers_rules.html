"An Eye For Numbers"

How good is your short-term memory? Well we are about to see how fast you can process data! Within 3 seconds, I will show you a 10-digit number and all you have to do, is memorize as much as you can. When that time is up, the number will vanish and then you will have to replicate as much as possible the number. Don't worry, you just have to correctly guess at least 6 numbers in the right position. Don't think it'll be that easy... I will also add 5 seconds for you to submit your guess. Failure to do so, will lead to your elmination. But your sucess will reward you 250pts!
